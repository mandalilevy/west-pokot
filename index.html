ENJOY BEAUTIFUL SCENERIES AT WEST POKOT COUNTY.

welcome to west pokot.

West Pokot is extremely endowed with natural resources ranging from scenic sites such as Mt Mtelo, Turkwel Gorge, Marich Pass, Tartar falls and sacred places which offer plenty of tourist activities.
For any tourist who loves to document an adventurous story to tell thereafter watching, There is also a privilege to interact with one of the indigenous Pokot culture practiced by the people of Tippet. These include, dance and song, rites of passage, and traditional Pokot delicacies.
